Te Whare Tapa Whā Well-being Plan

Taha Tinana (Physical Well-being)

  • Go for a swim or a walk before class twice a week.
  • Ensure 8 hours of sleep each night.
  • Incorporate one new vegetable into each meal.

Taha Whānau (Family Well-being)

  • Have dinner with my family every evening.
  • Catch up with a family member or friend once a week.

Taha Hinengaro (Mental and Emotional Well-being)

  • Practice guided meditation before sleep.
  • Journal twice a week to reflect on thoughts and feelings.

Taha Wairua (Spiritual Well-being)

  • Express gratitude each morning.
  • Do yoga outdoors to connect with nature.

Whenua (Connection with the Land)

  • Walk barefoot on the grass once a week.
  • Spend time outdoors appreciating nature.
